  <txtAbout>&lt;b&gt;About the game: &lt;/b&gt;

The player's task is move the Ball (the green sphere) into the basket.
If the ball falls down, the recovery system will teleport it`s to the start.

For resolve this difficult task need use the gravity points. Exists 3 types of Points:
- the normal - they gravitate the Ball;
- anti-gravity - they repel the Ball;
- pulsating - they gravitate the ball when it comes to point and push
when it moves away from the point. 
Each point has the power - ie how 
much it affects to the Ball. When changing power of the point are spent &lt;i&gt;"gravitons"&lt;/i&gt;.
 Amount of normal points is unlimited, antigravity and the pulse points you must collecting on level(prizes). Also by prizes
refilled &lt;i&gt;gravitons.&lt;/i&gt;
